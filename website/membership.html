How to Join HARC

  • Fill out the application form.
  • Mail or email the form to the club, or bring it to a meeting.
  • Once approved, pay your dues. Dues are $20/year cash or check, $21.50 via paypal.

    • Fiscal Year for the club is Apr 1 to Mar 31. Partial years are prorated.

    How to pay dues (for new and existing members)

  • Pay using PayPal
  • Come to a meeting and pay the treasurer.
  • Mail the dues and application (if required) to the club address
  • Contact the treasurer (listed on the officers page) via email for alternative payment options
