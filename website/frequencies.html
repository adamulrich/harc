Weekly Net

Every Tuesday @ 1930 hrs on NC7G

Net Script Net Checkin list Net Control Schedule

Every Tuesday evening at 1930 Hrs. local time, HARC and visitors come together on for a weekly check-in and conversations.

  • We meet on NC7G (2M) for a checkin.
  • We then meet on WA7ST (70 cm) for a brief checkin.
  • We then switch back to NC7G (2M) for an open ham related discussion.

    • Hams are encouraged to also check in via a WinLink message or form to WA7ST.

    The goal is to have fun and get new Ops on the air. All are welcome!
