ARDC Grant Project Summary

Name Status Priority Scope
Digital Radio Site Infrastructure Priority 1 Development of digital radio infrastructure at North Hill Water Tower (Des Moines) and SeaTac City Hall. Includes UHF, HamWAN, AREDN, Winlink, and repeater systems. Supports regional emergency communications.
Pre-Installed Antennas at Critical Facilities Priority 2 Install VHF/UHF antennas at 14 emergency sites (fire stations, police stations, city halls, water district). Includes Comet CX-333 and GP-15 antennas. Electrical contractor required for some installs.
Deployable AREDN Mesh Kits Priority 2 Three kits for Des Moines, SeaTac, and Burien. Includes mesh radios, switches, IP phones, and cables for emergency digital IP networks.
HF Digital Messaging Go-Box Priority 2 Two kits for Normandy Park and Burien. Supports Winlink via Amateur Radio or SHARES HF networks for statewide/national emergency messaging.
Digital Mobile Radio Equipment Priority 3 CS-800D DMR/FM radios for critical sites. Includes handheld Anytone 878UV radios for each city. Used for training, public service, and emergencies.
Winlink + VARA FM Messaging Equipment Priority 3 HYS TC-8900R quad-band radios and DRA-70 USB interfaces for digital messaging. Supports 6m, 144 MHz, and 440 MHz. Enables peer-to-peer Winlink across Highline area.
220 MHz Voice Radio Capability Priority 3 One radio kit per city to support WA State EOC directive for 220 MHz emergency communications. Reduces congestion on VHF/UHF bands.
Secure Storage Cabinets Priority 3 Cabinets for storing amateur radio equipment and supplies at critical sites. Includes branding for HARC and ARDC.
Computer Equipment Not Requested HARC will self-fund computers using surplus city laptops. No grant funds requested for this category.
Training & Outreach Initiatives No Funding Requested Includes recruiting CERT volunteers, connecting new hams to training and Elmers, Winlink and DMR clinics, and ongoing ACS/public service development.
